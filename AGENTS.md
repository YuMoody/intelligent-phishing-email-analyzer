# Project 
- Title: Intelligent Phishing Email Analyzer: Automated Assessment and Explanation
- Scenario: A Security Operation Center (SOC) reveives large volume of suspicious emails and needs to quickly determine whether they are phishing attempts and generate reports.
- Approach: Use and LLM (e.g., GPT-4o or Claude) to analyze email headers, body context, links, and attachment names, producing a phishing probability score and identifying indicators of compromise (IoCs)(e.g.,"sender impersonates the CEO" or "link redirects to a fake login page"). The system also generates natural-language explanations to assist junior analysts. 
- Key Technologies: Prompt engineering (few-shot learning + chain-of-thought promoting) email parsing libraries (e.g., email, eml-parser), optionial URL sandbox integration. 
- Deliverable: A web-based tool that accepts .eml files or pasted email content and outputs an analysis report.
